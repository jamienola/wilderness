Game Design Doc

Top-Down Minecraft with FTL Elements

Background
Ship crash lands on alien planet
No oxygen
Need to harvest alien resources and build
Start with bits of ship
Explore randomly generated map to find other ship parts and natural resources
Limited time outside because of oxygen tank limit
Pause anytime and give orders to units
No enemies during the day, but at night mobs come out
Create weapons to defend yourself
Combat works just like in FTL

Random resources - need to research with every new game. Random results plus random name pairings for each one.

Possible Names:
	No Help is Coming
